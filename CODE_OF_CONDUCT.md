# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

OpenOTP expects respectful, technically honest collaboration. Critique code, designs, and assumptions directly, but do not make it personal.

Examples of expected behavior:

- give clear technical feedback
- assume good intent
- disagree with reasoning, not people
- keep discussions focused on the work

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or insults
- discriminatory or exclusionary language
- bad-faith argumentation
- sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate these standards.

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and other project communication channels.
